#### 1700 N La Honda Dr

#### Escondido, CA 92027

* Take ramp onto I-15 N (Escondido Fwy), for 7.7 miles.   Take exit 32 onto CA-78 E., 1.3 miles
* Turn left onto N Broadway., 0.5 mi
* Turn right onto E El Norte Pkwy., 2.1 mi
* Turn left onto La Honda Dr.  At the top of the hill will be the park entrance
* After entering the park, follow past the Ranger Station, up to the Group Sites (25-29).  GPS `(33.157930, -117.050100)`
